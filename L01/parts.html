Principles of Web Design

Header

The top part of a website containing the logo and usually the site’s navigation menu.



Navigation

Links at the top of the page to help you find what you’re looking for.



Banner

A large image you see at the top of a web page that grabs your attention.



Content

All the elements used to communicate your message - text, images, video, audio, etc.



Footer

Performs the same function as the header except a footer is at the bottom of a page instead of the top. Contains contact info, privacy policy, terms of use, sitemap, social media icons and links to other important pages on your site.
